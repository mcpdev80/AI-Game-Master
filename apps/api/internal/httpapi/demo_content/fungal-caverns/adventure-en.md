# The Fungal Caverns — AI Game Master Guide (English)

This system-neutral one-page dungeon was created by Logen Nein. Use the cue `fungal_caverns_map` whenever the party enters a keyed cavern or asks to see their surroundings. Never reveal room numbers to players; the player map is deliberately unkeyed.

## Opening

The party reaches a small, sheltered cave. A massive boulder hides narrow cracks leading into a deeper cavern system. Ask what they do. Do not decide their actions for them.

## Keyed locations

1. **Sheltered entrance — cue: fungal_caverns_map.** A small cave suited for a campsite. A large boulder obscures tight cracks into the caverns.
2. **Great stalactite chamber — cue: fungal_caverns_map.** A vast chamber holds a massive stalactite ringed by fungi and a bottomless-looking pit. Movable boulders block the western tunnel; a ridge higher than two people hides space to the north.
3. **Scree and buried body — cue: fungal_caverns_map.** Stalactites rise from uneven ground covered by rubble. A body was buried hastily among the rocks.
4. **Webbed cavern — cue: fungal_caverns_map.** Strong webbing and cocoons cover the cavern. Each exploration turn, roll a d6: on 1, a small cluster of giant spiders attacks; on 6, a little treasure is found in a cocoon.
5. **Alchemist's shelter — cue: fungal_caverns_map.** A locked, strangely placed door protects a lived-in camp: fire ring, bedroll, stool, lightly locked chest with minor treasure, supplies, and an alchemist's worn workbench.
6. **Rust-red pool — cue: fungal_caverns_map.** Strange vegetation borders a deep rust-red pool. Treasure containing no iron lies below. Crumbling western masonry surrounds stairs descending into darkness.

## Cavern happenings — roll 2d6 each exploration turn

- 2: A dangerous ooze falls from the ceiling.
- 3: Two giant spiders hunt for food.
- 4: A piercing bestial screech echoes through the caverns.
- 5: Diseased, rotting bats swarm the area.
- 6: A hulking rust-red beast that dissolves iron lumbers nearby.
- 7: A wet, viscous mass drops from above.
- 8: Mushroom people on the warpath attack anything non-fungal.
- 9: A towering false stalactite lashes out with tendrils.
- 10: Rotting plants and fungal miasma become almost tangible.
- 11: A small goblin troop returns to its camp deeper in the ruin.
- 12: The cavern alchemist gathers specimens nearby.

## Facilitation notes

Keep rulings system-neutral unless the session's selected rules provide an answer. Prefer sensory description, meaningful choices, and consequences. When an encounter requires a roll, request the roll through structured output; never invent its result.
